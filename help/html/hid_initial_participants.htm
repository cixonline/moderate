Adding Initial Participants

Creating a New CIX Forum

If there are people on CIX who have expressed an interest in joining your new forum, you can add them to the participants list using the CIX Forums Participants menu command (or by pressing F4) with your forum highlighted in Ameol's topic list or a message window open in one of its topics.

Don't start adding people wholesale; not everyone wants to be involuntarily thrust into a new forum. It's polite to mail anyone you do add telling them they've been joined, too.
