Moderating a Forum

Forums are one of the main functions of CIX and each forum must have at least one Moderator. The Moderator's rôle is very important and, when carried out correctly, will make the forum work very well.

This part of the Moderate help file serves as a guide to Moderators - tips on how to run an efficient and successful forum, as well as how to perform the various Moderator functions.

What does moderating a forum involve?

The Forum Note

Looking after topics in your forum

Maintaining file lists

Adding and removing participants

Withdrawing messages
