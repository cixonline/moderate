Announcing Your New Forum

Creating a New CIX Forum

If your forum isn't closed confidential, you'll want to announce it's existence on CIX once you've created it.

Send a mail message to p_elphick, the moderator of the conference forum, so he can post a message for you in conference/announcements. Your mail should include:

* The name of your forum.

* Whether it's open or closed.

* A full description of the forum; what and whom it is for.

* A list of all your topics, again with a description of the purpose of each.

* And finally, the CIX nicknames of all the moderators of your forum.

You may also decide to post an announcement message in the noticeboard forum.
